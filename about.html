Process

This is an overview of the process of designing and developing by me (atleast the process I have been following till date for my past projects). The design process can be roughly phased as Research, Ideation, Designing and Prototyping and Development.

  • Research

    • This phase deals with user research to understand the target audience, their needs, expectations and views. Different user stories and scenarios is developed and then feature map is drawn to get a clear view of the requirements.

  • Ideation

    • This phase deals with developing user flows and journey to understand the interaction. Rough generic wireframes are made in order to visualise the structure and the layout.

  • Designing and Prototyping

    • Sketching of wireframes and overall design is done. The three Ps of designing (Paper, Pencil and Post-Its) comes handy for this job. Then high fidelity prototyping of the wireframes is done and the UX is designed using tools like Sketch and Adobe XD, accompained by InVision occassionally. The designs are iterated and tested before it started to get developed.

  • Development

    • Once the mockups are designed, development process starts. HTML, CSS and Javascript being my favorite technology to code the websites. I did develop few Android apps for personal projects which using XML and Java.
